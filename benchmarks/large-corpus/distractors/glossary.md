# Glossary

Cache: a faster copy of data. TTL: how long a cache entry is valid. Token: a
credential proving identity. Backoff: waiting longer between retries. Rate limit:
a cap on request volume. Failover: switching to a standby. Canary: a small first
slice of a release. Error budget: allowed unreliability.
