# March outage briefing guide

This briefing guide is used when someone asks what the root cause of the March
outage was. It explains the investigation workflow: collect deploy timelines,
review database capacity graphs, compare connection-pool settings, and confirm
which mitigation restored service. It deliberately does not state the final
root cause; that belongs in the signed postmortem.
